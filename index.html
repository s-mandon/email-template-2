Thank you for ordering your New FORD vehicle
  

Vehicle Delivery

Delivery information can vary with dates going backwards and forwards, but rest assured we will keep you fully updated along the way as accurately as we are able to.

Haynes Stock: Delivery dates agreed when you order your car.

Ford Stock: Approximately 4-6 weeks. We will update you when your car arrives in the UK, and then once it arrives in our PDI Centre.

Factory Order: Varies depending on model typically 8-20 weeks. Please speak to your sales executive for more details.

Method of Payment

BACS/CHAPS: 2 working days prior to collection (please ask your sales executive for bank details)

Debit Cards: Acceptable on the day

Cash: Only accepted between 9:00am to 4:00pm Monday - Friday (up to a total of £9,200 per vehicle)

Part Exchange?

Please remember to bring with you the following:

  • Current registration document (V5c) (IMPORTANT) - By law we cannot accept a part exchange without a current V5c.
  • MOT certificate (if applicable)
  • Service history
  • All keys
  • Locking wheel nut key
  • Your sales executive will complete an online notification to DVLA to advise of the transfer of the part exchange vehicle to us. Please do not contact DVLA yourself prior to bringing in your car to us.

Personalised Number Plate?

If you have a personalised plate on your car, please discuss the transfer process with your sales executive.

Signing Finance Documents

Prior to collection of your new vehicle, we will make an appointment at your convenience to complete your finance paperwork.

Please bring the following with you:

Driving Licence in your current address.

Bank/Credit Card with clear matching signature on back.

Bank Statement/Utility bill dated within the last three months (if requested)
